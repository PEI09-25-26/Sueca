## Background
    Deolinda is a Caretaker in a nursing home and she really enjoys card games. She always like to partake in discussions and watch the elderly play "Sueca" during her shifts.

Recently, she feels like the elderly have been wanting to make the matches they play of "Sueca" a bit more competitive, and she has been trying to organize a tournament, but the apps she uses for it don't help too much and using pen and paper in such a situation sometimes is not very handy either. On top of that, the bracket is often incomplete or neds to be paused due to complications.

## Goals & Needs
    She is looking for a way to better organize "Sueca" tournaments for the elderly in the nursing home she works at, while also being able to fix the issues that in making the bracket and filling it when problems show up.

## Pain Points & Challenges
    Most existing apps don't offer much help with organizing and participating in tournaments and the issue with the brackets with still remain unsolved.

## Motivation
    Deolinda wants to be able to organize tournaments better so she can provide the experience that the elderly and herself want to have in hosting/participating in them.
